"Education is a shared commitment between dedicated teachers, motivated students and enthusiastic parents with high expectations"

As a Principal of R.S.P.S, I extend hearty welcome to our school. R.S.P.S works on student centric approach which offers rewarding educational experiences to all who choose to focus on excellence. It aims at the commitments to nurture and equip young learners with the uncommon ability to become confident, sensitive, socially committed and to develop as an all round individuals.

Our commitment on holistic approach where each learner finds identity, meaning and purpose through connection to the natural world with spiritual values, keeps the flame of learning alive.

FROM DIRECTOR's DESK

We welcome you here, to a world of learning in enjoyable ways, to a world where every child is challenged to grow to his or her potential.

“Live as if you were to die tomorrow. Learn as if you were to live forever,” these words of Mahatma Gandhi have been my great guiding force and the thought I ardently wish to share with you dear children is: ‘The capacity to learn is a gift; The ability to learn is a skill; but the willingness to learn is a choice.”

Always remember that learning is a treasure which will follow its owner everywhere; so cherish this treasure and be life long learners.

Best Wishes and Blessings.

The R.S.P.S. Family: Building on Collective Strength

the Residential Sarvodaya public school Society has developed with a spirit and a vision. Today it stands as a citadel of learning and a harbinger of quality consciousness, committed to developing the minds and initiatives of children. The family is growing every day. The core schools have been set up by the Society and schools under aegis of the Residential Sarvodaya public school Society have been established globally in collaboration with concerned public, private sector undertakings and individual entrepreneurs. Believing that quality education is the high road to both individual and national progress, the Society has proved to be a significant trend setter in the field of school education.

Programs

Hosteller

Admission in this program means the student will live in our guidence and observation at our hostel. Guardian may take its child on leave on several occaisions, only after informing and following the necessary procedure.

Admitting in this program gets you following amenities:

  • Food and Lodging
    • Accommodation at Hostel
    • Food, 3 times (Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner) per day
  • Normal Schooling
  • Preparation for Competitive Entrance Exams
    • Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas
    • Sainik Schools
    • Netarhaat, Simultala

Day scholar

Admission in this program means the student will come from his/her home everyday to attend the classes

Admitting in this program opens door for the following amenities:

  • Normal Schooling
  • Preparation for Competitive Entrance Exams (optional)
    • Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas
    • Sainik Schools
    • Netarhaat, Simultala
